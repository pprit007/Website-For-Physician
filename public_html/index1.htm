If we think of the healthcare in America as a patient, the patient is quite sick. The symptoms are over-regulation, over-utilization, fraud and abuse, an obsolete payment system, defensive practice due to fear of lawsuits and many other similar ailments.
 

Code blue is called to resuscitate the patient and the code team led by Dr.Politician, Congress, Insurance Industry and AMA rush in. Dr.Politician confers with his team for a few minutes and then, decides to call consultants. He calls Dr.Canada, Dr.Europe and other experts for a second opinion. He finally declares with a somber face that he has made a diagnosis. The patient is suffering from an acute deficiency of regulations and an overdose of fraud and abuse. He prescribes more regulations, leading to higher doses of the treatment that has failed so far, and the patient subsequently dies.

In addition to staying out of micromanaging the healthcare, Government can do a great service by implementing the following:
•
Fund major prestigious medical institutions to establish evidence-based protocols and guidelines for disease management and treatment. Also sponsor a national system that is neutral and credible to give ratings to these protocols.
•
Fund a national system of networks so that each patient room in doctor’s offices and hospitals has a terminal to access these protocols.
•
Help the Genius of America to function freely and advance the medical knowledge to levels that no one else can reach. This process will keep prices of medical treatment the most competitive in the world and attract patients from other failed systems to our country leading to billions of dollars of a new source of revenue.
